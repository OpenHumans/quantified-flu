How am I supposed to remember when I fell sick?!

Here are some tips that might help you remember when you fell sick:

  1. Did you travel right before/after you fell sick? Are those travel dates on your calendar?
  2. Did you have to call in sick with your employer? Did you email/text them about it and can find those messages?
  3. Did you tweet about being sick in bed? Did you tell friends? Check if you can find text records of it, the Twitter search is great!
  4. Do you have tips to be added here? Be in touch with us and we'll add them!
